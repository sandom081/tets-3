Signature Towers

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most famous towers in the world. It is located in Paris, France, and stands 330 meters tall. The tower was built in 1889 as the entrance arch for the World's Fair held in Paris that year. Designed by the engineer Gustave Eiffel, the tower was originally meant to be a temporary structure. However, it became so popular that it was never taken down. Made from iron in a lattice design, the Eiffel Tower is a remarkable example of 19th-century engineering. Today, it is a symbol of Paris and attracts millions of tourists every year who come to enjoy its beautiful views and unique architecture. .
